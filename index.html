Discord Commands

  • *meowcount [player] - Shows the global meow count, or if a player is specified, shows their meow count
  • *meowlb - Displays the top 10 players with the most meows on the server
  • *onlineplayers - Generates an image showing all currently online players on Constantiam

  • Access Required - DM me on Discord or ping me in the Syndicate Discord, and I might give you access.
  • *whisper <ign> <message> - Sends a private message to a player in Minecraft
  • *loginwatch <add/remove> <ign> - Adds or removes a player from the login watch list. You will be notified when they log in

Minecraft Commands

  • +discord - Syndicate Discord invite link
  • +help - Links back to this
  • +meowcount [player] - Shows the global meow count, or if a player is specified, shows their meow count
  • +meowlb - Displays the top 5 players with the most meows on the server
  • +totalmeows - Shows the global meow count
